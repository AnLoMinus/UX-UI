# experience-craft Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Deliver premium, memorable experiences.
- Emphasize storytelling and emotional connection.
- Blend product and brand presence seamlessly.

## Visual System
- Palette: Deep neutrals with luxury metallic accents.
- Typography: Premium sans or serif pairing.
- Imagery: High-end photography and immersive visuals.
- Iconography: Refined icons with subtle detailing.

## Component Priorities
- Immersive hero sections, testimonial highlights, story modules.
- Premium CTAs, curated galleries, and showcase cards.

## Layout DNA
- Spacious, cinematic layouts with layered content.
- Strong focal points and generous negative space.

## Motion & Interaction
- Smooth, cinematic transitions and subtle parallax.
- Emphasize delight without hurting performance.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Maintain contrast on rich imagery and gradients.
- Provide reduced motion and readable overlays.

## Content Tone
- Elevated, confident, and aspirational.

## Quality Bar
- Every page feels cohesive and premium-grade.
