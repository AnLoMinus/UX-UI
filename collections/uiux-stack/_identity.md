# uiux-stack Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Compose interfaces from reusable block stacks.
- Encourage modularity and scalability in layouts.
- Support rapid recomposition without redesign.

## Visual System
- Palette: Neutral base with stack highlight accents.
- Typography: Straightforward sans, consistent scale.
- Imagery: Stack diagrams and modular tiles.
- Iconography: Block, layer, and stack symbols.

## Component Priorities
- Stack blocks, nested cards, dividers, drag handles.
- Expand/collapse sections and block metadata.

## Layout DNA
- Vertical rhythm and consistent spacing between modules.
- Support nesting with clear depth cues.

## Motion & Interaction
- Subtle lift and drop animations for stacking.
- Animated expand/collapse for modules.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear focus order across nested blocks.
- ARIA labels for expand/collapse actions.

## Content Tone
- Modular and action-oriented.

## Quality Bar
- Blocks remain interoperable across templates.
