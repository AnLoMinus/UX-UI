# designflow-ux Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Map navigation, information architecture, and flows.
- Support complex product ecosystems and multi-page journeys.
- Provide clarity in wayfinding and routing.

## Visual System
- Palette: Neutral base with navigation highlight accents.
- Typography: Clear sans with emphasis for navigation labels.
- Imagery: Sitemaps, flowcharts, and navigation diagrams.
- Iconography: Path, node, and navigation icons.

## Component Priorities
- Breadcrumbs, mega menus, sitemaps, navigation cards.
- Flow diagrams, route maps, and handoff tables.

## Layout DNA
- Hierarchical layouts with clear navigation zones.
- Consistent header/footer placement across pages.

## Motion & Interaction
- Motion guides direction and transitions between sections.
- Maintain consistency in page-to-page movement.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Ensure navigation is keyboard and screen-reader friendly.
- Provide skip links and structured landmarks.

## Content Tone
- Clear, structured, and directional.

## Quality Bar
- Every flow includes entry, path, and exit cues.
