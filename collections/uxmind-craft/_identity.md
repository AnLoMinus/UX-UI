# uxmind-craft Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Apply cognitive psychology to interface decisions.
- Reduce cognitive load and increase trust.
- Support clarity, intention, and decision-making.

## Visual System
- Palette: Calm neutrals with warm reassurance accents.
- Typography: Legible sans with strong emphasis styles.
- Imagery: Human-centric illustrations, cognitive maps.
- Iconography: Clarity and trust symbols.

## Component Priorities
- Guidance banners, reassurance chips, decision aids.
- Progressive disclosure panels and clarity tooltips.

## Layout DNA
- Clear grouping with cognitive chunking.
- Visual cues for priority and next action.

## Motion & Interaction
- Gentle feedback to reinforce choices.
- Avoid surprise transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Prioritize readability and error prevention.
- Provide clear recovery paths.

## Content Tone
- Empathetic, reassuring, and plain language.

## Quality Bar
- Every screen reduces ambiguity and supports confidence.
