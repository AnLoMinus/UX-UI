# uiux-canvas Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Provide expressive, creative layouts for storytelling.
- Allow asymmetric grids and visual experimentation.
- Support portfolio and campaign-style experiences.

## Visual System
- Palette: Bold accent colors layered over soft neutrals.
- Typography: Expressive display fonts with clean body text.
- Imagery: Mixed media, collage, and large hero visuals.
- Iconography: Playful icons and shape accents.

## Component Priorities
- Hero canvases, collage cards, immersive sections.
- Mixed media galleries and highlight modules.

## Layout DNA
- Asymmetric grids with intentional tension and balance.
- Overlapping content blocks and layered depth.

## Motion & Interaction
- Scroll-linked reveals, subtle parallax, and fluid transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Maintain readability and contrast despite creative layouts.
- Offer reduced motion alternatives.

## Content Tone
- Creative, bold, and narrative-driven.

## Quality Bar
- Visual experimentation stays within usability constraints.
