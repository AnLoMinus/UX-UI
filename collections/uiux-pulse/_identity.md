# uiux-pulse Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Capture attention through micro-interactions and motion.
- Add emotional feedback while keeping usability intact.
- Highlight transitions and state changes.

## Visual System
- Palette: Dark base with vibrant pulse accents.
- Typography: Modern sans with tight headings.
- Imagery: Motion streaks and kinetic gradients.
- Iconography: Animated icons and pulse indicators.

## Component Priorities
- Animated buttons, hover cards, toast notifications.
- Pulse indicators, progress animations, and micro-feedback.

## Layout DNA
- Clear content zones that allow motion to stand out.
- Avoid clutter to keep motion legible.

## Motion & Interaction
- Motion as feedback: pulse, bounce, subtle scale.
- Provide reduced motion settings.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Avoid flashing and ensure motion alternatives.
- Maintain focus visibility with motion off.

## Content Tone
- Energetic and engaging, but respectful.

## Quality Bar
- Motion always communicates state or feedback.
