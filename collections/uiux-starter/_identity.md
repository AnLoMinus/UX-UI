# uiux-starter Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Provide fast-start templates and minimal setup.
- Reduce decision fatigue with sensible defaults.
- Serve as a friendly on-ramp for new teams.

## Visual System
- Palette: Soft neutrals with a single accent color.
- Typography: Straightforward sans with short scale.
- Imagery: Light illustrative accents.
- Iconography: Simple icons for quick recognition.

## Component Priorities
- Basic nav, cards, form fields, alerts, lists.
- Simple onboarding checklists.

## Layout DNA
- Simple grids with predictable sections and minimal variation.
- Emphasis on clarity over experimentation.

## Motion & Interaction
- Minimal transitions, instant feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear focus states and legible defaults.
- Larger touch targets to reduce setup issues.

## Content Tone
- Friendly, encouraging, and concise.

## Quality Bar
- Templates are usable with minimal customization.
