# uiux-kit Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Deliver a toolbox of interchangeable components for rapid builds.
- Emphasize consistency, modularity, and simple assembly.
- Keep onboarding friction low for new teams.

## Visual System
- Palette: Clean neutrals with a bright primary accent for actions.
- Typography: Friendly sans with uniform sizing and strong baseline grid.
- Imagery: Functional diagrams and light pattern textures.
- Iconography: Filled/outline pairings for quick state swaps.

## Component Priorities
- Buttons, inputs, dropdowns, toggles, tooltips, pagination, badges.
- Utility blocks: cards, dividers, tabs, and alerts.

## Layout DNA
- Modular blocks sized to 4/8px spacing system.
- Layout recipes included for common screens.

## Motion & Interaction
- Subtle transitions with emphasis on discoverability.
- Focus on hover/active states to teach usage.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- All controls labeled, touch targets 44px minimum.
- Tab order and focus visibility baked in.

## Content Tone
- Helpful and instructional. “Do this next” microcopy.

## Quality Bar
- Components ship with usage notes and edge states documented.
