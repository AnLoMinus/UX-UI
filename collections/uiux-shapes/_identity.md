# uiux-shapes Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Highlight geometry-driven UI components.
- Use shape language to structure content and navigation.
- Explore modular shapes without sacrificing clarity.

## Visual System
- Palette: Clean neutrals with geometric accent colors.
- Typography: Strong, simple sans for clarity.
- Imagery: Geometric patterns and shape motifs.
- Iconography: Shape-based icons and outlines.

## Component Priorities
- Geometric cards, polygon buttons, shape badges.
- Shape-based tabs and decorative dividers.

## Layout DNA
- Grid aligned with shape modules and consistent aspect ratios.
- Use of diagonals or curves as accents only.

## Motion & Interaction
- Shape morphing for microinteractions.
- Hover states emphasize geometry.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Ensure shape-based cues are paired with text labels.
- Maintain minimum touch targets.

## Content Tone
- Precise and visual, with concise labels.

## Quality Bar
- Shapes remain consistent across breakpoints and themes.
