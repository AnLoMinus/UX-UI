# uiux-designcraft Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Prioritize typography, spacing, and content clarity.
- Serve editorial and content-heavy experiences.
- Maintain rhythm and readability across long-form layouts.

## Visual System
- Palette: Soft neutrals with subtle accent highlights.
- Typography: Primary serif or refined sans with strong typographic scale.
- Imagery: Editorial photos, line illustrations.
- Iconography: Minimal icons supporting text-first layouts.

## Component Priorities
- Rich text modules, pull quotes, content cards, lists.
- Type scale previews and spacing tokens.

## Layout DNA
- Wide margins, clear columns, and vertical rhythm.
- Adaptive typography for different breakpoints.

## Motion & Interaction
- Gentle fades and focus on reading comfort.
- Avoid distracting animation in long-form pages.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Readability-first: line length, contrast, and spacing.
- Provide dyslexia-friendly options if needed.

## Content Tone
- Thoughtful, refined, and descriptive.

## Quality Bar
- Typography scale and spacing tokens are meticulously documented.
