# uiux-launchpad Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Build high-conversion landing experiences.
- Make CTAs dominant and persuasive.
- Support campaigns, product launches, and waitlists.

## Visual System
- Palette: High-contrast accents with trust-building neutrals.
- Typography: Bold headings and readable body text.
- Imagery: Product showcases and benefit illustrations.
- Iconography: Feature icons and benefit markers.

## Component Priorities
- Hero CTAs, value props, pricing tables, FAQ blocks.
- Testimonials, trust logos, and comparison cards.

## Layout DNA
- Strong vertical flow with conversion-focused hierarchy.
- Clear sectioning and repeated CTAs.

## Motion & Interaction
- Attention cues on CTAs, subtle hover animations.
- Minimal distractions in core conversion path.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Ensure CTAs and forms are keyboard-friendly.
- Provide accessible pricing and plan details.

## Content Tone
- Persuasive, energetic, benefit-focused.

## Quality Bar
- Each page includes at least three conversion touchpoints.
