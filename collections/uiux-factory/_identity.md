# uiux-factory Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Enable high-volume production of variants and themes.
- Maintain quality while scaling component permutations.
- Support product lines with shared foundations.

## Visual System
- Palette: Neutral base with modular theme accents.
- Typography: Stable base with tokenized weight variants.
- Imagery: Production lines, modular tiles.
- Iconography: Variant markers and batch indicators.

## Component Priorities
- Variant selectors, theme switchers, batch controls.
- Component inventory cards and release tags.

## Layout DNA
- Matrix layouts to display variants side-by-side.
- Systematic spacing and alignment for comparisons.

## Motion & Interaction
- Quick toggles, instant state swaps, low-friction switching.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear labeling for variants and theme contrast checks.
- Ensure parity across variants for keyboard users.

## Content Tone
- Efficient and operational. Focus on repeatability.

## Quality Bar
- Every variant documented with usage and contrast checks.
