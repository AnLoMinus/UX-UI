# uiux-booster Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Enhance speed and perceived performance.
- Emphasize efficiency and quick workflows.
- Optimize for high-frequency interactions.

## Visual System
- Palette: Bright accent paired with fast, clean neutrals.
- Typography: Strong hierarchy with compact labels.
- Imagery: Minimal, performance-focused.
- Iconography: Sharp, quick-action icons.

## Component Priorities
- Inline loaders, quick actions, compact menus.
- Shortcut hints, fast search, and command overlays.

## Layout DNA
- Compact, efficient spacing with strong alignment.
- Scannable layouts for rapid decision-making.

## Motion & Interaction
- Snappy animations under 150ms.
- Emphasize immediate feedback and micro-confirmations.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Ensure motion doesn't reduce readability.
- Maintain keyboard shortcuts and focus order.

## Content Tone
- Action-driven, concise, and performance-first.

## Quality Bar
- Every flow highlights time-saving affordances.
