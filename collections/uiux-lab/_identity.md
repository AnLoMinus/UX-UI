# uiux-lab Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Incubate experimental layouts, interactions, and emerging patterns.
- Encourage exploration while documenting learnings.
- Provide safe guardrails for testing new ideas.

## Visual System
- Palette: Vibrant gradients with contrasting dark base.
- Typography: Mixed weights with expressive headings.
- Imagery: Abstract visuals, kinetic shapes.
- Iconography: Experimental icons and playful accents.

## Component Priorities
- Prototype widgets, interaction demos, feedback loops.
- Concept cards and experiment logs.

## Layout DNA
- Flexible grids with irregular spacing for exploration.
- Highlighted experiment zones.

## Motion & Interaction
- Bold transitions, layered motion, and hover experiments.
- Include motion-tuning guidelines.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Motion reduction alternatives and clear focus states.
- Avoid flashing or high-frequency motion.

## Content Tone
- Curious and exploratory. Encourage hypothesis-driven tests.

## Quality Bar
- Every experiment includes learnings and next steps.
