# uiux-blueprints Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Establish precision grids and layout rules for scale.
- Make measurement, spacing, and alignment transparent.
- Enable teams to blueprint screens before styling.

## Visual System
- Palette: Cool neutrals with blueprint blues and measurement highlights.
- Typography: Technical sans with consistent tabular numerals.
- Imagery: Grid overlays, rulers, and schematic line art.
- Iconography: Thin-stroke icons with modular geometry.

## Component Priorities
- Grid overlays, spacing tokens, layout cards, breakpoint matrices.
- Rulers, baseline grids, and annotation tags.

## Layout DNA
- 8pt spacing scale with visible constraints and margins.
- Focus on predictable, repeatable structure.

## Motion & Interaction
- Minimal motion; use fades to reveal annotations.
- Transitions prioritize clarity over delight.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast-safe annotation colors and readable labels.
- Clear sizing for measurements and tokens.

## Content Tone
- Technical, precise, reference-style copy.

## Quality Bar
- All layouts include explicit margins, gutters, and breakpoints.
