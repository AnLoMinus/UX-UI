# uiux-flow Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Map user journeys and onboarding flows.
- Provide clarity across multi-step experiences.
- Reduce drop-off with guidance and progress cues.

## Visual System
- Palette: Calm blues and supportive greens.
- Typography: Friendly, clear type with step emphasis.
- Imagery: Journey maps and milestone icons.
- Iconography: Step and progress indicators.

## Component Priorities
- Steppers, progress bars, timeline cards, checklists.
- Guided tour panels and contextual tips.

## Layout DNA
- Linear flow layouts with clear next actions.
- Persistent progress indicators.

## Motion & Interaction
- Smooth transitions between steps.
- Positive reinforcement on completion.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear labels for steps and progress states.
- Ensure stepper is navigable via keyboard.

## Content Tone
- Supportive and guiding. Emphasize next-step clarity.

## Quality Bar
- Each flow includes reassurance, progress, and exit paths.
