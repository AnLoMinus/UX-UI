# humanui-craft Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Design for human comfort, clarity, and accessibility.
- Prioritize inclusive design and readability.
- Support diverse user abilities and contexts.

## Visual System
- Palette: High-contrast base with warm, friendly accents.
- Typography: Large, readable type with generous spacing.
- Imagery: Human-centric photography and soft illustrations.
- Iconography: Friendly, rounded icon style.

## Component Priorities
- Accessible forms, helper text, clarity banners.
- Friendly cards, large buttons, and guidance panels.

## Layout DNA
- Spacious layouts with clear hierarchy.
- Comfortable line length and spacing.

## Motion & Interaction
- Motion is minimal and calming.
- Avoid movement that could distract or overwhelm.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA/AAA targets where possible.
- Strong focus indicators and screen reader hints.

## Content Tone
- Warm, empathetic, and supportive.

## Quality Bar
- Every component passes accessibility checks.
