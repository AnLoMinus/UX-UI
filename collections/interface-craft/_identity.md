# interface-craft Identity

## Strategic Focus
- Build practical, task-focused interfaces.
- Optimize for productivity and clarity.
- Support complex data without overwhelming users.

## Visual System
- Palette: Neutral grays with purposeful action accents.
- Typography: Functional sans with compact scale.
- Imagery: Minimal; focus on data clarity.
- Iconography: Utility icons with clear meaning.

## Component Priorities
- Action bars, data tables, filters, and bulk tools.
- Forms, inline edits, and quick actions.

## Layout DNA
- Structured layouts with consistent action zones.
- Dense but scannable grid.

## Motion & Interaction
- Motion is utilitarian and limited.
- Emphasis on fast feedback for actions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear focus order and ARIA labels for tables.
- Support keyboard shortcuts.

## Content Tone
- Direct and operational.

## Quality Bar
- Task completion prioritized over visual embellishment.
